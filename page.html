Umwelt

From the German word meaning “surrounding world,” refers to one's species-specific perception of reality. In this exercise, we mutate to experience from the perspective of a pet.

From memory, sketch a pet.
Below your sketch, write its needs and frustrations.
Give it a name if you'd like.

Candle

To set the vibe. Consider when.

From memory, sketch your candle.
Using three words, describe its scent.

Materials
